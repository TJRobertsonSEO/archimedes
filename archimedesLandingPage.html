Program Overview

At Archimedes Academy, we've redesigned education for gifted students who love learning for its own sake. Our summer program offers a glimpse into our distinctive approach: deep reading of seminal texts, meaningful math instruction that builds true understanding, and writing that develops clear thinking. Students engage with big ideas across disciplines in an intellectual salon atmosphere that pushes their capabilities further than they thought possible.

About Our Founder

Lamson Nguyen

Founder, B.A. Mathematics, M.A. Teaching (in progress)

After experiencing firsthand how traditional education often fails to challenge gifted students, Lamson spent a decade researching educational methods and developing our interdisciplinary curriculum that pushes students to their intellectual limits.

Drawing from his background in mathematics from Cal State Fullerton and his ongoing studies in education, Lamson has created a unique learning environment that combines rigorous academic standards with innovative teaching approaches.
